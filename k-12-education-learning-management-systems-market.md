<p><p>The K-12 Education Learning Management Systems (LMS) market can be divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based LMS solutions are hosted remotely, offering scalability, accessibility, and reduced IT maintenance costs. They facilitate easy updates and collaboration across locations. In contrast, on-premise LMS solutions are installed locally within a school's infrastructure, providing greater control and customization but requiring significant investment in hardware and ongoing maintenance. Each type serves different institutional needs and resource availability.</p></p>
