<p><p>The embedded hardware market for Edge AI includes several key types: GPUs (Graphics Processing Units) are versatile and powerful, suited for parallel processing tasks; VPUs (Vision Processing Units) are optimized for low-power vision applications, enhancing real-time image processing; FPGAs (Field Programmable Gate Arrays) offer flexibility and reconfigurability, allowing for custom hardware acceleration; while ASICs (Application-Specific Integrated Circuits) provide maximum efficiency for specific tasks but lack versatility. Each type caters to distinct needs within Edge AI applications, balancing performance, power consumption, and adaptability.</p></p>
