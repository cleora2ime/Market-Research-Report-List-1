<p><p>The medical biocompatible materials market includes various types, with Polyether ether ketone (PEEK) and Polyphenylsulfone (PPSU) being prominent. PEEK is known for its excellent mechanical properties, chemical resistance, and compatibility with body tissues, making it suitable for implants and surgical devices. On the other hand, PPSU offers high thermal stability and hydrolytic resistance, ideal for medical applications requiring sterilization. Both materials are increasingly used in developing advanced medical devices, enhancing patient safety and treatment outcomes.</p></p>
