<p><p>In the industrial market, aluminum foil is categorized into three types based on thickness: heavy gauge foil, medium gauge foil, and light gauge foil. Heavy gauge foil is used for demanding applications requiring durability, such as heavy-duty packaging and thermal insulation. Medium gauge foil strikes a balance, serving various packaging needs while maintaining flexibility. Light gauge foil is primarily utilized for less rigorous tasks, including wrapping and baking, offering cost-effectiveness and ease of use for lighter applications.</p></p>
