<p><strong>The Stainless Steel Scrubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Winding Scrubbers</li><li>Woven Scrubbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel scrubber market primarily consists of two types: winding scrubbers and woven scrubbers. Winding scrubbers are made from wound stainless steel strands, providing a flexible and effective cleaning solution for tough surfaces. They are ideal for pots and pans, effectively removing stubborn residues. Woven scrubbers, on the other hand, feature interlaced stainless steel fibers, offering a more gentle approach to cleaning while retaining durability. Both types are popular for their resistance to rust and longevity, making them essential in kitchens.</p></p>
